# Online-Teaching-Site
**EduVibe** is a modern, immersive online learning platform designed to connect eager learners with expert mentors through cutting-edge courses and live interactive sessions.

The platform features a sleek dark-themed interface with vibrant gradient accents, creating a premium, tech-forward atmosphere that appeals to serious learners. At its core, EduVibe offers three distinct learning paths—Beginner, Professional, and Expert—each tailored to different skill levels with personalized progress tracking and AI-powered recommendations.

Key highlights include **live streaming classes** with real-time viewer interaction, an extensive catalog of hand-picked courses spanning design, development, business, and marketing, and a thriving community where students share progress, collaborate on projects, and network with peers. The platform emphasizes mentorship, featuring profiles of industry leaders from top companies like Google, Meta, and Spotify who provide specialized guidance.

With functional features like global search, shopping cart integration, wishlists, and personalized dashboards tracking learning streaks and course completion, EduVibe delivers a complete educational ecosystem that goes beyond passive video content to foster genuine skill development and professional growth.
